What is Mini Vyapar?

Mini Vyapar is a lightweight and user-friendly POS (Point of Sale) system crafted especially for local shopkeepers. It helps you manage daily sales, inventory, and expenses digitally - without any complex setup or extra costs.

Whether you're running a small store in a city or a rural area, Mini Vyapar gives you everything you need to print receipts, track sales, and manage stock right from your computer. It's simple, offline-ready, and perfect for first-time users!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mini Vyapar free to use?

Yes, Mini Vyapar is completely free with all features included. We may offer premium add-ons in the future but core functionality will remain free.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely! Mini Vyapar works offline and stores all your data locally. Internet is only needed if you want to backup your data to cloud.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

You can install it on multiple devices, but data won't sync automatically between them. You'll need to manually export and import data.

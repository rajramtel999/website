What is Mini Vyapar?

Mini Vyapar is a lightweight and user-friendly POS (Point of Sale) system crafted especially for local shopkeepers. It helps you manage daily sales, inventory, and expenses digitally — without any complex setup or extra costs.

Whether you're running a small store in a city or a rural area, Mini Vyapar gives you everything you need to print receipts, track sales, and manage stock right from your computer. It's simple, offline-ready, and perfect for first-time users!

Key Features:

  • Create and store digital receipts
  • Track daily sales and inventory levels
  • Record and monitor expenses
  • Easy-to-use, clean interface
  • Works offline using local storage
